Frequently Asked Questions

Is HealthSync really free?

Yes! HealthSync is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features.

Do I need any special equipment?

No equipment required. Just access HealthSync through your web browser and you're ready to go.

How accurate are the calculations?

We use scientifically validated formulas (Harris-Benedict, Mifflin-St Jeor) for BMR and calorie calculations.

Can I use this if I'm over 30?

While designed for ages 18-30, the system works for anyone. However, consult a doctor for personalized medical advice.

Is my data stored?

No. Your information is only used for calculations and is not stored without your explicit consent.

Do I need to download an app?

No downloads needed. HealthSync works directly in your web browser on any device.
